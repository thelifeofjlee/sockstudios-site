## Security Deposit

The $300 security deposit will be held by Socks Studios and released within **5 business days** after the event, provided that:

- No damage was caused to the venue, furniture, equipment, or property
- No house rules were violated (see House Rules section)
- The space was returned to its original layout and condition
- No additional cleaning beyond normal use was required

If damage or violations occur, Socks Studios reserves the right to deduct the cost of repairs, deep cleaning, or replacement from the deposit. If costs exceed the deposit amount, Client agrees to pay the difference within 10 days of being invoiced.

## House Rules

By signing this agreement, Client agrees to comply with and enforce the following rules for all guests:

1. **No smoking or vaping inside the studio.** The space is cannabis-friendly but all consumption must take place outside or with proper ventilation in designated areas.
2. **No food or drinks outside the dining/seating area.** Spills on hardwood floors, furniture, or equipment may result in deposit deductions.
3. **No tape, nails, screws, or adhesives on walls, ceilings, or floors** without prior written approval.
4. **No standing on furniture** at any time.
5. **Maximum occupancy:** 100 guests.
6. **The studio must be returned to its original layout** before the booking end time, including moving any furniture that was rearranged.
7. **Load-in and load-out must occur within the booked time window.** Early arrival or late departure is not permitted without prior written approval and may result in additional hourly charges.
8. **Garbage must be bagged and placed in designated bins** before leaving. Excessive mess may result in deposit deductions.
9. **No alterations to the lighting, electrical panels, or HVAC systems** without prior approval.
10. **Respect the building and neighboring tenants.** Although there are no noise restrictions, Client is responsible for their guests' conduct in common areas and entryways.

## Cancellation Policy

- **7 or more days before the event:** 50% of the rental fee is refunded. Cleaning fee and security deposit are refunded in full.
- **Less than 7 days before the event:** No refund on the rental fee or cleaning fee. Security deposit is refunded in full provided no damage occurred.
- **No-shows:** No refund of any fees. Security deposit is refunded if space is left undamaged.

Cancellations must be submitted in writing via email to sockstudios.la@gmail.com.
